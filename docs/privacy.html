Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App. You can delete your account at any time from Settings, which deletes your profile and associated App data from our systems, subject to any legal retention requirements.

Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information, including encryption in transit, hashed passwords, access controls, and monitoring. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we continually improve our safeguards.

Your Choices
